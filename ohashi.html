Katelyn Ohashi

Born: April 12th, 1997
Height: 4'10"
College: University of California

Biography

Katelyn Ohashi is an American gymnast who competed for UCLA. She's known for her floor routines that incorporate popular dance moves. In 2019, a video of her perfect 10 floor routine went viral, making her a global sensation. She was born in Seattle, Washington on April 12, 1997 and started gymnastics at age 3. She competed on the US National Team in 2011. Uses her platform to speak out on issues of self-worth, inclusiveness, and empathy.

Achievments

  • Earned her first perfect 10 at the 2019 Collegiate Challengey
  • Competed for UCLA, where she became a six-time All-American
  • Won the 2013 American Cup, defeating Olympic gold medalist Simone Biles

Famous Quote:

"I know how important it is to stay humble"
