Simone Biles

Born: March 14th, 1997
Height: 4'8"
College: University of the People

Biography

Simone Biles is an American Gymnast. She has won more medals than any other gymnast is history. When she was young she was adopted by her grandparents and grew up in Texas. She started gymnastics at 6 years old. She is married to an NFL play Jonathan Owens. She won her first world title in 2013 and won five medals in the 2016 Rio Olympics. She won a silver medal in the team event and a bronze medal for the balance beam in the 2020 Tokyo Olympics. Recently, she has won four medals at the 2024 Summer Olmypics in Paris. She is considered on of the best gymnasts in history.

Achievments

  • She has written an auto biography
  • She has 11 Olympic Medals
  • Simone has 30 World Championship medals
